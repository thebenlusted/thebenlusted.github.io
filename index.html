// skills //

sound design

designing everything from weapons to monster roars, I do it all. Using techniques like synthesis and layering recorded material, I strive to achieve the perfect sound for each project.

music

producing music for myself and for video games is a huge part of my work. After 11 years, I am still writing songs and uploading them here. So, go check them out!

implementation

a vast knowledge of middleware tools like Wwise and FMOD. I enjoy designing systems in these tools and translating them into the engine. I have plenty of experience creating events for different things and adding snapshots to fit the environments of the project, and also adding adaptive music!

game development

i also have great experience working in game engines. I have programmed, designed sound and music, created 3d/2d art, and built entire worlds in Unity, Unreal Engine, GameMaker, and have had experience in CryEngine and Frostbite.

// who am i? //

i am a sound designer based in Vancouver, Canada. I have been designing sounds since 2011, and have thoroughly enjoyed it. I have had the chance to work on titles like Need For Speed, and the hit Xbox title Shotgun Farmers.
