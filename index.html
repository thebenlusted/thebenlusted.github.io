Who Am I?

My name is Ben Lusted, and I am a sound designer for video games. I have been designing sounds for video games since 2011, and have worked on many different game-jam projects, and bigger ones such as Shotgun Farmers and Need for Speed: Heat. I am from Vancouver, British Columbia, and am always looking to work with passionate teams to bring a voice to their vision.
